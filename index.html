Welcome to The Green Room

The Green Room Matcha Bar is a bright, feel-good cafe space made for slow mornings, study sessions, catch-ups with friends, and your favorite matcha fix. With a soft, inviting atmosphere and handcrafted drinks, it’s the kind of place you’ll want to stay awhile.

About

A matcha bar made to feel like your favorite little escape.

The Green Room blends clean design, cozy energy, and playful charm into a cafe experience that feels both refreshing and familiar. Created with students and young creatives in mind, the space is designed for everything from study dates and solo resets to quick coffee runs with friends.

From signature matcha drinks to a warm, welcoming atmosphere, Green Room is all about creating a space that feels aesthetic, comfortable, and easy to come back to again and again.
